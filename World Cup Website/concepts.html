Key Concepts That are Good to Know

  1. FIFA: The global governing body of soccer that organizes the World Cup.
  2. VAR: Video Assistant Referee technology that helps ensure fair play.
  3. Golden Boot: Award given to the top goal scorer.
  4. Offside Rule: Prevents players from gaining unfair attacking advantages.
  5. Penalty Shootout: Decides tied matches during knockout rounds.
  6. Group Stage: The first round of the World Cup tournament.
  7. Knockout Stage: The single-elimination rounds leading to the final.
