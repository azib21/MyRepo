World Cup Legends

These players left a lasting mark on the history of the World Cup.

  • Pelé (Brazil): The only player to win three World Cups (1958, 1962, 1970).
  • Diego Maradona (Argentina): Known for his “Hand of God” and creativity.
  • Lionel Messi (Argentina): Led Argentina to victory in 2022.
  • Kylian Mbappé (France): Young superstar who scored a hat-trick in the 2022 final.
